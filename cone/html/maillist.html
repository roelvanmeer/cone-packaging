Mailing list

courier-cone - the mailing list for Cone-related questions and comments. Its name is due to the fact that Cone is a parallel project, split off from Courier, and Sourceforge mailing lists have names that always begin with the main project's name. Go to http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/courier-cone to subscribe to courier-cone, or send a message to <courier-cone-request@lists.sourceforge.net> and put “subscribe” in the subject line. Search the list archives at http://markmail.org/browse/net.sourceforge.lists.courier-cone
